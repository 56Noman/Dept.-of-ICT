Overview of the Department

The genesis of the Department of Information & Communication Technology can be traced back to 1998 when it was first instituted as a constituent of the Faculty of Applied Science and Technology, keeping in step with the astounding progress in information technology witnessed during that era. Nonetheless, as time progressed, it underwent a transfer of jurisdictional authority and found its abode in the Faculty of Engineering and Technology. It was a visionary move that recognized the pivotal role of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in national economic and human development worldwide, spurred by the rapid advancements in information technology. As a pioneer among Bangladeshi universities, the department has successfully completed 24 years of operation, marking a significant milestone in its journey toward academic excellence.

The department's foundation is based on cutting-edge and fundamental principles, constantly adapting to the ever-changing technological landscape. With access to the latest software, hardware, electronic, communication laboratories, and a multimedia library, the department incorporates technical innovations into its educational curriculum annually.

In this era of globalization, the department's primary objective is to train and nurture skilled information experts and technologists who can propel the country's progress. Graduates from this esteemed department have garnered a reputable image both nationally and internationally, holding esteemed positions.

Every year, the ICT department orchestrates a variety of technically sophisticated activities to enrich the knowledge and expertise of its students. Among these activities are:

Workshops and Seminars: Workshops and Seminars: The department conducts workshops and seminars on emerging ICT topics, inviting industry experts and renowned professionals to share their insights. These events expose students to the latest trends, technologies, and best practices in the field, fostering their growth and development.

Coding Competitions: The department hosts coding competitions to cultivate innovation and problem-solving abilities. These events challenge students to devise imaginative solutions within a specified timeframe, promoting teamwork and refining their programming skills.

Industry Visits: The department arranges visits to leading IT companies, research institutes, andtechnology hubs, offering students a firsthand look at real-world ICT implementations. Students caninteract with professionals, gain practical insights into the industry's operations, and broaden theirhorizons.

Internship Programs: The department collaborates with organizations to provide internship programs for students, offering hands-on experience and enabling them to apply their theoretical knowledge in practical settings. This approach bridges the gap between academia and industry, equipping students with valuable skills and experience.

Research Projects: Students are encouraged to undertake research projects in various ICT areas, withthe department providing guidance and resources to support their endeavors. This approach fosters a culture of innovation and contributes to the field's advancement.

Guest Lectures: The department invites eminent professionals and researchers as guest lecturers to share their expertise and experiences with students. These lectures broaden students' perspectives,expose them to diverse viewpoints and provide valuable insights into the practical aspects of ICT.

Technology Exhibitions: The department hosts technology exhibitions where students showcase their projects and innovations. These exhibitions serve as a platform for students to demonstrate their technical skills, creativity, and entrepreneurial spirit, inspiring them to push the boundaries of ICT.

Networking Events: The department facilitates networking events that bring together students, alumni, industry professionals, and faculty members. These events provide opportunities for students to establish connections, seek mentorship, and explore potential career paths in ICT, empowering them to excel in this dynamic field.

By offering these technically sophisticated activities every year, the ICT department ensures that its students receive a comprehensive and practical education, equipping them with the skills and expertise required to succeed in the ever-evolving world of ICT.
